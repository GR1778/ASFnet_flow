   Looking forward, this limitation reveals a promising direction. Temporal
information across consecutive frames provides crucial motion cues that help
resolve challenging cases and stabilize pose trajectories. Building on the en-
couraging results of our initial multi-frame extension, future work will focus
on developing temporally enhanced versions of our core modules (e.g., T-
AMS and T-UDE) to explicitly model human motion continuity. We believe
this will lead to more robust pose estimation in complex, in-the-wild envi-
ronments, and we hope our work inspires further research in this direction.
